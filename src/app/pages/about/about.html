Why We Started ErgoMaster

Like many, our founder spent 10+ hours daily sitting in an uncomfortable chair, causing chronic back pain and reduced productivity. After years of searching for the perfect ergonomic solution without success, he decided to design one.

What started as a personal project evolved into a mission: create premium, affordable ergonomic chairs that genuinely improve people's lives and work quality.

Today, over 1000 customers trust ErgoMaster for their workspace comfort and wellbeing.
