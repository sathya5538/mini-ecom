Frequently Asked Questions

What is the delivery time?

We deliver across India in 3-5 business days. Free express delivery available on orders above ₹4,999.

Can I return the product?

Yes! We offer a 30-day return guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund or replace your order.

What's included in the warranty?

Our 1-year warranty covers manufacturing defects. Normal wear and tear are excluded.

Do you offer custom colors?

Currently, we offer black and grey. Custom colors available for bulk orders upon request.

How do I care for the chair?

Clean the mesh with a soft brush and mild soap. Avoid harsh chemicals. Check all bolts monthly.

Is assembly required?

Yes, basic assembly is required (10-15 minutes). Instructions and tools are included in the box.
